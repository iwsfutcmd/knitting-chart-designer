How to use:

1. Set your desired grid size and click "Create Grid"

2. Select a color from the palette

3. Click to paint a cell, or drag to paint multiple cells

4. Click again with the same color to erase it back to white

5. Use Undo/Redo buttons or keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Shift+Z)

6. Click "Clear Grid" to reset everything
