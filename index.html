How to use:

1. Set your desired grid size and click "Create Grid"

2. Select a color from the palette

3. Click on any square to paint it, or click and drag to paint multiple squares

4. Click a square again (with the same color selected) to erase it back to white

5. Use Undo/Redo buttons or keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Shift+Z) to navigate your changes

6. Click "Clear Grid" to reset everything
